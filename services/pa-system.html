Public Address System

Zone-wise IP announcements, emergency broadcast, and background music for large facilities.

Clear communication, exactly where it's needed

Our public address systems let you deliver announcements to the right zone at the right time — whether that's a routine update to one floor or an emergency evacuation message across an entire campus.

Built on IP-based networks, our PA systems scale easily across large facilities and integrate directly with fire alarm panels to trigger automatic emergency broadcasts the instant a threat is detected.

Beyond emergencies, the same system handles scheduled announcements and background music, making it a flexible communication backbone for offices, retail spaces, and industrial sites alike.

  • Zone-wise targeted announcements across the facility
  • Automatic emergency broadcast on fire alarm trigger
  • IP-based network for easy scaling and expansion
  • Background music and scheduled announcement playback
  • Integration with building management and fire systems
