About JMS Nextech

Securing Tomorrow, Today

For over a decade, JMS Nextech has been the security systems partner businesses, government bodies, and industrial facilities trust to protect what matters most.

Our Story

Built on trust, engineered for reliability

JMS Nextech was founded with a simple goal: make advanced security technology accessible, reliable, and genuinely easy to live with. What began as a small CCTV installation team has grown into a full-scale systems integrator serving enterprise, government, and industrial clients across 50+ cities in India.

Today we design, install, and maintain complete security ecosystems — from surveillance and access control to fire safety, AI analytics, and building automation — all backed by certified technicians and a rapid-response support team.

We believe security is never "one and done." Every project starts with a thorough site assessment and ends with a long-term partnership, including 24/7 monitoring support and scheduled maintenance built around your operations.
